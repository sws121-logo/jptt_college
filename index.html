About JPTT College

Our Journey Since 2017

Jehal Prasad Teachers Training College was established on 2nd May 2017 with the vision to provide quality teacher education in the Nawada region. Our first academic session commenced in 2018, and since then we have been dedicated to shaping competent and compassionate educators.

Located in Pathra Englishodhanpur, Nawada (805123), our college offers state-of-the-art facilities including well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, and experienced faculty members committed to student success.

We believe in holistic development and provide various cultural and extracurricular activities to ensure our students grow not just academically but as well-rounded individuals.
