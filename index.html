About JPTT

Jehal Prasad Teachers Training College (JPTT) was established on 2nd May 2017 with a vision to provide quality teacher education. Our first academic session commenced in 2018, and since then we have been committed to nurturing future educators.

Our college is located in Pathra Englishodhanpur, Nawada, Bihar, with postal code 805123. We offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip aspiring teachers with the knowledge and skills needed for modern educational environments.

College Highlights
