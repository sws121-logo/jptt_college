About JPTT College

Jehal Prasad Teachers Training College (JPTT) was established on 2 May 2017 with the academic session commencing in 2018. Located in Pathra Englishodhanpur, Nawada, Bihar (805123), our institution is dedicated to providing quality teacher education.

Our mission is to empower future educators with innovative teaching methodologies and practical training that prepares them for the challenges of modern education systems.

We offer various teacher training programs designed to meet the evolving needs of the education sector, with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty members.
