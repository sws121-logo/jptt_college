About JPTT College

Jehal Prasad Teachers Training College (JPTT) was established on 2 May 2017 with the vision to provide quality teacher education. The first academic session commenced in 2018.

Our college is located at Pathra Englishodhanpur, Nawada, Bihar - 805123. We are committed to nurturing future educators with a perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

JPTT College offers a conducive learning environment with well-equipped laboratories, a rich library, and experienced faculty members dedicated to student success.
